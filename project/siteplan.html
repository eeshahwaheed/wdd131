Tales & Tea

Tagline: Sip, Read, and Share

Optional Domain Availability: talesandtea.com

Site Purpose

The site serves as a cozy hub for book lovers, offering information on how to join the club, a schedule of events, resources for readers, and a space for reviews and ratings from members.

Planned Site Content

  • Home Page: Introduction to the club and joining information.
  • Events Page: Schedule of book discussions, author events, and tea-themed activities.
  • Resources Page: Links to local libraries, bookstores, and reading guides.
  • Reviews Page: Member reviews, ratings, and subscription options.

Scenarios

  • How can I join the Tales & Tea club?
  • What events are scheduled for this month?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #6A5ACD (Used for headings and links)

Secondary Color: #FF6347 (Used for accents and buttons)

Typography

Font Choices:

  • Headings: Georgia, serif
  • Body Text: Arial, sans-serif

Wireframe

A simple wireframe for the homepage layout will include:

Desktop View:

  • Header with logo and navigation
  • Main introduction section
  • Upcoming events section
  • Resources links section
  • Footer with contact information and social media links

Mobile View:

  • Collapsible menu for navigation
  • Main introduction section at the top
