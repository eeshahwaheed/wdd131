Tales & Tea

Tagline: Sip, Read, and Share

The name "Tales & Tea" evokes a cozy atmosphere where book lovers can share stories while enjoying a warm cup of tea. It reflects the inviting spirit of the club, creating a memorable and engaging space for readers to connect.

Site Purpose

The site serves as a cozy hub for book lovers, offering information on how to join the club, a schedule of events, resources for readers, and a space for reviews and ratings from members.

Scenarios

  • How can I join the Tales & Tea club?
  • What events are scheduled for this month?

Typography
